Guacamole

This delicious dish is perfect for topping on your favorite mexican recipes, or scooping with tortilla chips.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados- peeled, pitted and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic

Steps

  1. Peel and pit the avocados.
  2. In a medium bowl, add the avocados, lime juice and salt. Mash to desired consistency.
  3. Add in onion, cilantro, tomatoes and garlic. Stir well.
  4. Refrigerate for 1 hour for best flavor
  5. Serve and enjoy!
